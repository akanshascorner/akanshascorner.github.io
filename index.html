Roster Management System

Many organizations keep much of their roster information and other relevant information in disparate locations in an unorganized fashion. We reached out to the Stargazers Astronomy Club to see if they had any need for any software or programs. One of their top requests was a roster management system in order to track membership information, take attendance, and send out emails to club members. We hope to deliver on this shared vision and be able to create a generalized platform so that any registered student organization can access and utilize the system in order to streamline executive board functions.
